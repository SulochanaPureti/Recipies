Dosa recipe: Learn how to make

Ingredients

  • 1/2cup Urad dal/ skinless blackgrams
  • 2cups Rice
  • 1/2 t spoon fengreek seeds
  • Salt as requried
  • Water as nedeed
  • Oil

Instructions: Preparation for Dosa batter

1. Firstly wash the the Urad dal with fengreek seeds then soak them together 6h

2. Next wash the rice and soak them 6h

3. After that take the mix jar or grinder to make smooth batter

4. Ferminate this batter whole night

5. To make the dosa, check the batter thinckness and if you need add water, then add some salt mix them

6. Heat the dosa pan, sprikle littel water then pour the full ladle batter on center of th pan, spred this to pan cirularly.

7.Drizzle oil on the dosa and cook this until brown or golden color, and then flip it if you want or take off this from pan

8. serve this with coconut chunty with ginger pickle.
